Tempai

Your AI Meteorologist

HotTopic is a project developed for CSE 368, focused on predicting future weather patterns over incredibly long time frames—think decades or even centuries. Our goal is to leverage advanced AI models and an AWS-powered backend to provide accurate, long-term weather forecasts based on specific geographic locations.

Location Info:

To start we ask that you enter a valid longitude and latitude in the United States followed by a valid date.

Text to Speech:

By having a text to speech as a part of our application we hope the predictions we generate will have more weight by hearing a voice convey them.

Generating a Prediction:

Our meteorologist is able to casually report the weather including things like temperature, and a number of other things included in a casual weather report.
